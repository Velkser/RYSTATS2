## Copywriting Principles Used

The assistant emphasized several conversion-copy principles:

### 1\. Lead with user benefit

Avoid wording that describes internal design values. Use wording that explains what the user gains.

Example:

```text
Clean motion
```

is weaker than:

```text
Smart routes
```

\---

### 2\. Use concrete product language

Words like `cheap flights`, `real Ryanair prices`, `map`, `price`, `distance`, and `€/km` are more concrete than abstract phrases like `strategy`, `tactical`, or `clean motion`.

\---

### 3\. Make CTAs action-oriented

The strongest CTA is:

```text
Find cheap flights
```

because it tells the user exactly what will happen after clicking.

\---

### 4\. Reduce internal jargon

Terms like `preset`, `scan state`, and `tactical motion` are more suitable for developers than end users.

\---

### 5\. Highlight sharing as a product loop

Because the user said sharing is important, one hero feature card should explicitly mention sharing.

Recommended share-focused wording:

```text
Follow, compare, and share the best flights
```
